    Bowa

    Bowa is a concept for a publication and brand based around the idea of breaking the stigmatization of marijuana use to an audience of women 18-45. Using a comprehensive visual system delivering information regarding cannabis, its culture, and the industry surrounding it; Bowa would attempt to attain this purpose.
