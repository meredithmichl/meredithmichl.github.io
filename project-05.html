    The Huddle

    The Huddle is an idea of an online community for women and girls active and interested in different athletics. The site not only would serve as a platform to exchange ideas, information, and advice between users, but also a source of current and archival sports articles. The design of the reading space places recent and historical women's athletic news back to back, keeping the audience up to date on current events with a dose of historical context.
