About Me

Hi, I'm Meredith 

I am an effective brand strategist and a bold user-oriented designer, with a passion for refined typography. In my free time, I mentor middle school girls learning to skateboard in Los Angeles. My interest in design started while reading print magazines like Nylon growing up and later Thrasher. I am an alumna of Otis College of Art and Design (BFA in Communication Arts). Currently located in Los Angeles, I am available for full-time and freelance opportunities.
