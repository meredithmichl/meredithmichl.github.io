The Shadow Project
Experiential

The Shadow Project is a conceptual public service installation piece in Los Angeles that encourages driver awareness of pedestrian crossings by using signage that resembles street signs, pamphlets, and painted human shadows at the end of intersections to grab drivers' attention.
