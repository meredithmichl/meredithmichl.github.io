    The Shadow Project

    The Shadow Project is a conceptual public service installation piece in Los Angeles that would work in conjunction with the city's current Vision Zero project. The installation is meant to promote driver awareness of pedestrian crossings using signage that resembles street signs with informative take-away pamphlets. The campaign is intended to encourage the idea of invisible crosswalks through its design and creating invisible pedestrians by painting human shadows at the end of intersections.
