    Coyl

    Coyl is an idea for a technology-based brand that would offer a bracelet and accompanying app. The bracelet would feature tech that would calculate a users heart rate and other possible health variables, tracked in the app. The brand design is evocative of the idea that the collecting of this data gives the user a broad insight and connection to their health.
