    Mrs. Eaves

    This project consists of a 24x36-inch poster featuring Mrs. Eaves, the typeface designed by Zuzana Licko in 1996. The design celebrates the typeface's elaborate and abundant ligatures while highlighting Mrs. Eave's delicate nature and bold impact.
